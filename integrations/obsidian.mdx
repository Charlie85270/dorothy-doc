Automatically sync documents from Dorothy's Vault to your Obsidian vault. Keep your AI agents' knowledge accessible in your personal knowledge management system.

## Why Sync with Obsidian?

Dorothy's Vault stores documents that your agents create and reference. By syncing with Obsidian, you can:

- **Browse agent knowledge** in a familiar interface
- **Search across everything** using Obsidian's powerful search
- **Link agent outputs** to your own notes and projects
- **Keep a permanent record** of what your agents have produced

## Setting Up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Obsidian settings in Dorothy">
    Go to **Settings → Obsidian** in Dorothy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your Obsidian vault path">
    Enter the file path to your Obsidian vault directory on your computer (e.g., `/Users/you/Documents/MyVault`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable auto-sync">
    Toggle on **Auto-sync**. Dorothy will now automatically export new Vault documents to your Obsidian vault.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the sync">
    Create or update a document in Dorothy's Vault, then check your Obsidian vault to confirm it appears.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How It Works

- New documents created in Dorothy's Vault are automatically exported as Markdown files to your Obsidian vault.
- The folder structure from Dorothy's Vault is preserved in Obsidian.
- Tags are converted to Obsidian-compatible format (e.g., `#tag-name`).
- Sync happens whenever a document is created or updated in Dorothy.

<Note>
Sync is one-directional: from Dorothy's Vault to Obsidian. Changes you make to the synced files in Obsidian will not be reflected back in Dorothy.
</Note>

<Tip>
Create a dedicated folder in your Obsidian vault for Dorothy exports (e.g., `Dorothy/`). Set this folder as the sync target in Dorothy settings to keep agent documents separate from your personal notes.
</Tip>

<Accordion title="What file formats are supported?">
  All documents are exported as standard Markdown (`.md`) files. Attachments and images referenced in documents are also synced to the vault.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I sync multiple Obsidian vaults?">
  Dorothy supports syncing to one Obsidian vault at a time. If you use multiple vaults, choose the one where you want agent documents to appear.
</Accordion>
